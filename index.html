About Affinity Tax & Consulting LLC

Cory Roberts, CPA

Cory Roberts, CPA gained his accounting experience as a Tax Manager with Deloitte & Touche (now Deloitte) where he spent over eight years in both the Boston, MA and Las Vegas, NV offices working with multi-national corporations, high net-worth individuals, and the various business and investment vehicles utilized by such clients. For the next nine years Mr. Roberts has had his own accounting practice where he continues to consult with domestic and international investors specializing in Income Tax Planning and Consulting. In 2012 Mr. Roberts also took on the responsibilities of Chief Financial Officer and Chief Operations Officer of the Yamagata Enterprises Family Office, which has been a client of his accounting practice since 2003. He has since opened a new business with Michelle Tyson and works as the Senior Partner at Affinity Tax & Consulting.

Mr. Roberts earned his Masters of Accountancy and Bachelor’s of Science degrees from Brigham Young University and is fluent in Portuguese.

Michelle Tyson, EA, CPA

Michelle Tyson, EA, CPA has worked in public accounting since 2004. She received a Bachelor’s in Accounting in 2007, became an Enrolled Agent in May of 2012, and received her MBA in 2013. Mrs. Tyson received her CPA licensure in 2015. Mrs. Tyson has worked with Mr. Roberts as the Tax Manager at Vranes, Coffman, Roberts CPAs for 4 years. She is now partnered with Mr. Roberts at Affinity Tax & Consulting. Affinity Tax & Consulting has been in operation since May 2015.

Prior to working in the accounting field she was in restaurant management. Mrs. Tyson has lived in Las Vegas for since 2011, and prior lived in Toledo, Ohio for 13 years where she also worked in public accounting.

As tax planning and preparation specialists, we know how to maximize your tax-saving opportunities while staying within the guidelines provided by the Internal Revenue Code. We provide our clients with a Big Four perspective with small firm personalized service. We're here to help you get there!

At Affinity Tax & Consulting we understand how each aspect of your tax plan affects your financial story. With our expertise we are able to help you develop a detailed tax strategy that identifies and capitalizes possible opportunities in order to save on local, state and federal taxes.

Non-Profit Tax Services:

The partners at Affinity Tax & Consulting have worked with many non-profit organizations over the years, including private and publicly supported foundations. We know that protecting your tax exempt status is very important to you. The IRS uses very specific revenue and expense classifications to determine if your oganization will retain its tax-exempt status. Therefore, it is imperative that you build your accounting system around these revenue and expense classifications.

With our experience and knowledge, we can help protect your organization's tax-exempt status and prepare your annual Form 990 and/or 990-T to be filed with the IRS. We will also assist you in determining if your organization may be subject to unrelated business taxes, and if so, we can help to minimize your exposure to this tax.
